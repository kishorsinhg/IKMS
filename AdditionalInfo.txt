Intelligent Knowledge Management System
1.This is generic platform and can be used by domian and industry by configuring required information.
2.Knowledge sources can be document, email, notes, records
3.Document can be auto indexed and classified using AI
4.Human in loop is needed to reindex or correction
5.There is a concept of Master Entity and Related Entity. While defining entity I can decide which is master and which is related entity.For e.g. In case of Insurance Customer is master Entity and Policy,Beneficiary etc.. are related entity. 
6.Master Entity is customer profile where all documents, emails, notes,tasks,history appears in a single view of that customer.We store and show all interaction with customer in this view
7. When ever we receive document it goes through Index-->Classify-->Human Review if not less confidence-->Link to Customer-->Store-->Retrieve
8.OCR/ICR via AI mostly Mistral cloud.
9.Considering simple monolithic application
10. RAG search is required
11. Entity,Metadata for Entity, Document Type, metadata for Document type are configurable entity.
12. Indexer, Processor, Supervisor, Administrator are four roles
14. Indexer can index document which are not auto indexed.
15. Processor can search, retrieve, view customer and document but hide PII information
16. Supervisor can do everything what Processor can do plus view PII information
17. Administrator has system access and configure configurable information.
18. auditing, governence,guardrail,security everything as it is no change.
19. Want to make application simple and can be used by small legal, insurance boker like company having 20-25 users.
